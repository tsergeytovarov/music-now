Stream Recommendations

Tips and tricks to make your performance awesome

Must

  • Bandwidth: ensure that you have a good connection, preferably Wi-Fi
  • Battery: make sure that your Phone or iPad is connected to an electric outlet, or its battery is charged enough
  • No calls:

If you are using Wi-Fi:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Turn on Airplane Mode
  3. Turn on Wi-Fi

If you are using 3G:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap Phone
  3. Tap Call Forwarding
  4. Turn on Call Forwarding
  5. Type in the Phone Number you wish your calls to be forwarded to

Optional but Important

Tripod: put your iPhone or iPad on a tripod, or on any stable surface for a better video quality

Camera positioning: stream in a vertical position, as there is no option for horizontal video on our website yet

Clean lens: wipe the lens for a better video quality
